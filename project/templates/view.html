View
Elevation (elev): This angle determines how much the view is tilted above or below the xy-plane.
When elev is 0, the viewpoint is level with the xy-plane. Positive values of elev tilt the view upward, and negative values tilt it downward.
Azimuthal (azim): This angle, on the other hand, represents the rotation around the z-axis. When azim is 0,
the viewpoint is aligned with the positive x-axis. As azim increases, the view rotates counterclockwise around the z-axis.
